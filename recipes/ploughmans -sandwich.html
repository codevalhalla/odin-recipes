Ploughman's Sandwich

Ploughman's sandwich is a classic British ploughman's cold meal

This meal is often eaten by laborers' midday break when working in the fields.

A very popular lunch served in most of UK restaurants and pubs. Tasty and healthy meal.

Ingredients:-

  • 2 slices crusty bread
  • 2 slices crusty bread
  • handful of arugula
  • 2 sweet gherkin pickles, sliced
  • 3 slices smoked ham
  • 2 slices white Cheddar cheese
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon whole grain mustard

Directions to cook:-

  1. Lightly butter both slices of bread.

  2. Layer the arugula, pickles, ham, Cheddar, and tomatoes on one slice of buttered bread. Spread the mustard on the remaining buttered bread slice, and place on top. Cut sandwich in half, and serve.
